📈 Monthly Revenue & Order Volume (2016–2018)

Time Series
Revenue grew 10× from Sep 2016 to Aug 2018. Clear Black Friday spike in Nov 2017. Both revenue and order count move in near-perfect lockstep, indicating consistent average order value.

🏆 Top 10 Product Categories

Revenue
Beleza Saude (Health & Beauty) leads at R$1.26M. Top 5 categories account for ~42% of all platform revenue.

🗺️ Sales by Brazilian State

Geographic
São Paulo (SP) dominates with 41,125 orders — 3× more than RJ. Southeast region drives >60% of all orders. North & Northeast are untapped markets.

💳 Payment Method Distribution

Preferences
Credit card dominates at 73.9%, reflecting Brazil's installment payment culture. Boleto bancário is second at 19% — a uniquely Brazilian cash-payment method.

🚚 Delivery Time by Category

Logistics
Heavy/large items (furniture, office) take 25+ days to deliver. Categories with long delivery times correlate directly with lower customer review scores.

⭐ Customer Satisfaction vs Delivery Speed

Satisfaction
Fast delivery (0–7 days) → 4.42★ vs very slow (22+ days) → 3.12★. Delivery speed is the single strongest predictor of customer review score on the platform.

💰 Price Analysis by Product Category

Pricing
PCs & Computers have the highest avg price at R$1,098. Wide min-max ranges in electronics indicate a mixed product tier — budget accessories to premium devices. Watches & Gifts (Relogios Presentes) show large price variance driven by luxury items.

💡 Key Findings & Recommendations

Finding 01

Revenue grew 10× in 2 years. The Nov 2017 Black Friday peak shows seasonal demand is a major lever. Increase inventory and marketing spend in October–November.

Finding 02

SP/RJ/MG account for >65% of orders. North and Northeast regions are severely underserved. Regional logistics partnerships could unlock significant new demand.

Finding 03

Credit card = 73.9% of payments. Optimise instalment UX and consider adding Buy-Now-Pay-Later options to capture customers who avoid Boleto friction.

Finding 04

Delivery time is the #1 driver of review score. Fast (0–7d) orders score 4.42★ vs 3.12★ for slow (22+d). Investing in express fulfilment directly improves ratings.

Finding 05

97% of customers are one-time buyers. Customer retention is the largest untapped growth lever. Loyalty programmes and re-engagement emails could 2× lifetime value.

Finding 06

Health & Beauty leads revenue despite not being the highest-priced category — driven by high repeat purchase frequency. It's the ideal category for subscription models.
