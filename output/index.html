Performance Metrics

Monthly Revenue & Order Volume Trend

Time Series
Insight: Revenue experienced extreme growth, scaling 10× from Sep 2016 to Aug 2018. A clear Black Friday demand spike occurred in Nov 2017. Order volume scales linearly with revenue.

Top 10 Categories by Revenue

Sales
Insight: Health & Beauty leads all categories with R$1.26M, closely followed by Watches & Gifts. The top 5 categories collectively command ~42% of platform revenue.

Sales Distribution by State

Geography
Insight: São Paulo (SP) is the core market, generating 3× more orders than Rio de Janeiro (RJ). The Southeast region dominates, highlighting untapped potential in the North.

Payment Method Preferences

Finance
Insight: Credit cards capture 73.9% of all transactions, strongly driven by Brazil's installment culture (parcelamento). Boleto bancário remains a critical secondary option (19%).

Customer Review Score vs Delivery Speed

CX Metrics
Insight: Direct inverse correlation. Fast deliveries (0-7 days) yield a premium 4.42★ rating, while very slow deliveries (22+ days) crash to a critical 3.12★.

Price Analysis by Category (Avg + Variance)

Pricing
Insight: PCs command the highest average price at R$1,098. The immense min-max ranges in categories like Watches & Gifts indicate a highly diverse product mix from entry-level to luxury tiers.
Executive Summary

Strategic Recommendations

01

Optimise for Q4 Seasonality

Data confirms a massive Black Friday spike in November. Recommendation: Front-load inventory acquisition and scale up marketing spend starting late October to capture peak demand.

02

Expand Logistics Infrastructure

Heavy items (Furniture) take 20+ days to arrive, directly killing review scores. Recommendation: Partner with specialized heavy-freight couriers and establish distribution hubs closer to the North/Northeast.

03

Prioritise Retention Marketing

A staggering 97% of users make only a single purchase. Recommendation: Deploy automated post-purchase email flows, loyalty point programs, and retargeting ads to increase Customer Lifetime Value (LTV).

04

Double Down on Health & Beauty

Despite a lower average price point, this category generates the highest total revenue due to volume. Recommendation: Introduce "Subscribe & Save" models for replenishable cosmetic goods.
